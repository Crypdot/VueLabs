Commodore 64

History
Commodore 64, abbreviated C64, was introduced in 1982 and gained worldwide popularity. It is still the most sold computer in the world. (image of C64, download a copy to local site from http://commons.wikimedia.org, include copyright notice as a caption)

Current status
There's still a wide community of C64 enthusiasts. There are numerous C64 emulators, such as CCS64 (link to http://www.ccs64.com/) and Frodo (link to http://frodo.cebix.net/).
